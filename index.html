Property Registration & Legal Services

Complete end-to-end property registration services:

  • Sale Deed Registration — Legal ownership transfer with full documentation support.
  • Power of Attorney Registration — Secure authorization for property handling.
  • NRI Power of Attorney Adjudication — POA adjudication, stamp duty compliance and legal validation for NRIs.
  • Gift Deed — Transfer property legally within family members.
  • Release Deed — For family settlements or ownership release cases.
  • MODT (Memorandum of Deposit of Title Deed) — Loan mortgage registration support for banks.
  • Discharge / Reconveyance Deed — Document release after loan closure.
  • BDA / BBMP E-Khata Transfer — Khata transfer and related documentation.
